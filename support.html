1. Getting Started

System Requirements

  • macOS 13 (Ventura) or later
  • Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) or Intel Mac
  • Internet connection required only for AI extraction and MerusCase features

Installation

Download GLF WC PD Rater from the Mac App Store. The app installs like any standard macOS application.

First Launch

  1. End-User License Agreement (EULA) — Read and accept the license agreement to proceed. This appears only on first launch.
  2. Welcome Walkthrough — A brief guided tour introduces you to the main features. You can revisit this anytime from Settings.
  3. Start Rating — You are taken to the PD Rating Calculator, ready to begin. No account or sign-up is required for Free-tier features.

Setting Up an AI Provider Pro

To use AI-powered report extraction, connect an AI provider in Settings → AI Provider:

  1. Open Settings (gear icon in the top bar).
  2. Tap AI Provider.
  3. Choose your provider: Anthropic Claude or OpenAI GPT-4o.
  4. Enter your API key. You can obtain one from console.anthropic.com (Anthropic) or platform.openai.com (OpenAI).
  5. Tap Save. The app will validate your key immediately.
Note AI calls use your API key and are billed directly by the provider. The app never stores or transmits your key to any third party.

Setting Up MerusCase Pro

To pull case data from MerusCase and save PDFs back to cases:

  1. Open Settings → MerusCase.
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions to log in to MerusCase in your browser.
  3. Navigate to the API token page in your MerusCase account settings.
  4. Generate a new OAuth bearer token.
  5. Copy the token and paste it into the app.
  6. Tap Connect. The app will verify the token and confirm the connection.
Tip Your MerusCase token is stored locally on your Mac. If the token expires, simply repeat the process to generate a new one.

Configuring Firm Branding Pro

Customize your PDF exports with your firm's identity in Settings → Branding:

  • Logo — Upload your firm's logo (PNG or JPEG recommended).
  • Firm Name — Appears on the header of exported PDFs.
  • Tagline — Optional subtitle displayed below the firm name.
  • Attorneys — List attorney names that appear on the letterhead.
  • Display Mode — Choose between Logo Only or Logo + Text layout.

3. PD Rating Wizard (Step by Step)

Step 1: Upload (or Skip)

You can upload a medical-legal report (PDF) for AI extraction Pro, or skip this step and enter data manually.

Step 2: Review & Edit

Case Information

Enter the key case details:

  • Worker Name
  • Employer
  • Date of Injury (DOI) — format: MM-DD-YY
  • Date of Birth (DOB) — format: MM-DD-YY
  • Claim Number
  • WCAB Number

Occupation Search

Type to search across 1,800+ occupations organized into 45 PDRS groups. You can:

  • Click a specific occupation for the exact variant
  • Click the group header to select the entire occupational group

Body Parts

Add each injured body part with the following fields:

Field Description
Body Part Name of the injured body part (e.g., Lumbar Spine)
WPI% Whole Person Impairment percentage
Pain Add-On Additional percentage for pain (0–3%)
Impairment Number AMA Guides impairment code (e.g., 15.03.02.05)
Industrial % Percentage of impairment caused by industrial injury (apportionment)
A/G Toggle Enable Almaraz/Guzman alternative rating when applicable
Impairment Level Conversion If your report provides Upper Extremity, Lower Extremity, hand, foot, or digit impairment values instead of WPI, use the built-in converter to translate them to Whole Person Impairment.

AWW & TD Rate

Enter the Average Weekly Wage (AWW) or Temporary Disability (TD) Rate. These fields are synced — type in either field and press Enter to auto-calculate the other. If left blank, AWW defaults to the minimum of $290/week.

Step 3: Calculate

Choose your combination method:

  • CVC (Combined Values Chart) — The standard California method.
  • Additive — Simple addition of individual PD values.

The app instantly calculates the combined PD rating, applies any applicable adjustments (age, occupation, apportionment), and produces the final permanent disability percentage.

Step 4: Results

The results screen displays:

  • Rating string for each body part (the full formula breakdown)
  • Individual and combined PD percentages
  • PD indemnity — total dollar amount
  • Weekly rate and total weeks
  • Life Pension — automatically calculated when PD is 70% or above, including lifetime value with SAWW/COLA adjustments
COLA Rate Adjustment For Life Pension projections, the app applies Cost of Living Adjustments based on the State Average Weekly Wage (SAWW). You can adjust the COLA rate used in the projection.

From the results screen, you can:

  • Copy rating strings to clipboard (one-tap)
  • Export to PDF Pro
  • Export to Word (RTF) Pro
  • Save to MerusCase Pro
  • Save case locally for later recall

5. AI-Powered Report Extraction Pro

Upload a medical-legal report (QME, AME, or treating physician) and let AI extract all relevant data automatically.

How It Works

  1. Upload PDF — Drag-and-drop or click to select a medical report PDF. Scanned documents are automatically processed with OCR.
  2. AI Analysis — The AI reads the report and extracts: body parts, WPI percentages, AMA Guides impairment codes, pain add-on values, apportionment determinations, and Almaraz/Guzman analysis.
  3. Review Results — Extracted data populates the rating form. Review and edit any values before proceeding to calculation.

Supported AI Providers

Provider Model Best For
Anthropic Claude (Sonnet) Detailed extraction with strong reasoning about complex medical reports
OpenAI GPT-4o Fast, reliable extraction with broad medical knowledge
Extraction Caching The app caches extraction results. If you upload the same PDF again, it will return the cached result instantly without making another AI call, saving you time and API costs.

Guzman Analysis

When the AI detects findings that may qualify for an Almaraz/Guzman alternative rating, it will flag them and provide a dual rating comparison showing both the standard and alternative ratings side by side.

7. MerusCase Integration Pro

Seamlessly connect to your MerusCase account to pull case data and save completed rating PDFs directly to case files.

Connecting to MerusCase

  1. Open Settings → MerusCase.
  2. Click the link to open MerusCase in your browser.
  3. Log in to your MerusCase account.
  4. Go to your account settings and find the API / Integration section.
  5. Click Generate Token (or copy your existing bearer token).
  6. Return to the app and paste the token into the Bearer Token field.
  7. Click Connect. The app will verify access and display a success confirmation.

Pulling Case Data

Once connected, you can search for cases by name or case number. The app will pull:

  • Worker name and contact details
  • Employer information
  • Claim and WCAB numbers
  • Date of injury and date of birth

Saving PDFs to Cases

After generating a PDF rating report, click Save to MerusCase to upload the document directly to the associated case file in your MerusCase account.

Token Expiration MerusCase bearer tokens can expire. If you see a connection error, simply generate a new token and reconnect in Settings.

8. Vigil/Kite Group Builder Pro

The Vigil/Kite group builder lets you organize body parts into groups and control how they are combined, following the Vigil v. WCAB framework.

How to Use

  1. Create groups — Organize body parts into logical groups (e.g., orthopedic injuries in one group, psychiatric in another).
  2. Assign body parts — Drag or assign each body part to the appropriate group.
  3. Set combination method per group — Choose Additive (within group) or CVC (within group).
  4. Set between-group method — Choose how groups are combined with each other (Additive or CVC).
  5. Calculate — The app calculates the final PD using your specified grouping and combination strategy.
Why Vigil/Kite? Under Vigil v. WCAB and Kite v. WCAB, attorneys can argue for specific combination methodologies that may yield a higher PD rating for the injured worker. This tool lets you model those arguments precisely.

11. Tips & Notes

Date Format All dates in the app use MM-DD-YY format (e.g., 03-15-24 for March 15, 2024).
AWW Default If Average Weekly Wage is left blank, it defaults to the statutory minimum of $290/week.
AWW and TD Rate Sync The AWW and TD Rate fields are linked. Type a value in either field and press Enter — the other will calculate automatically.
Text Size Use the + and − buttons in the top bar to increase or decrease the text size throughout the app.
Extraction Caching AI extraction results are cached locally. Uploading the same PDF a second time returns the cached result instantly, saving API costs and time.
Privacy All PD calculations are performed locally on your Mac. AI calls are made directly to your chosen provider using your own API key. The app collects no personal data and transmits nothing to Gimbel Law Firm or any other third party.
Disclaimer This app is a calculator tool only. All ratings should be verified by a qualified professional. The results do not constitute legal advice.
